About Me

Hi, I am Juthika and I love making websites! I’m currently learning Full Stack Web Development.


But, I wasn't always Coding for a living. After working as a Middle School teacher for a few years, a series of interesting events inspired me to get into coding, and since then I’m simply hooked onto it! I taught myself the fundamentals of web development and built my first ever website for a family member. This motivated me to pursue my passion for coding and make a career as a Web Developer.


When I’m not coding, I love to go hiking, cooking and eating my way through the places I visit. In my spare time, I follow the Rifle Shooting sport and have myself represented India at International level.


You can browse through my projects on the Portfolio page. If you like my work, then feel free to reach out by filling out the Contact form and I'll get back to you as soon as possible. Looking forward to connecting with you.
